One-week sprint to build a fraud detection system

This was a final project with just one week to complete. Originally I wanted to analyze endgame gamemodes character usage databases for gacha games. unfortunately, those databases aren't open to the public, I ended up settling on a credit card fraud database.

The goal was to build a machine learning model that could detect fraudulent transactions. Conceptually it wasn't too difficult overall, but thats because the information needed to categorize the transacations were already available.

If I had picked a different topic I might've had to do more research and data collection to find more custom parameters to analyze with. machine learning is conceptualy not too complicated, but it requires a lot of data and math to do. Dont get me started on the syntax use.

KarpTime App Concept

UX/UI Design ‚Ä¢ Business Strategy ‚Ä¢ Figma

Social media time management app idea

This was a full app concept we developed as a team around gamifying social media time management. The idea was to help people manage their screen time through game-like rewards and social features. We created a complete business model with user accounts, coin systems, avatar customization, leaderboards, even premium subscriptions.

Made Figma mockups, put together presentations, researched the ethics and social impact. It was fun thinking through all the business and design challenges, we ended doing a little presentation for the class shark tank style.

The basis of the project was something similar to sweatcoin, a app that tracks the number of steps you take and turns it into a digital currency to spend. It's pretty interesting overall, honestly I could probably use a app like this with how much I scroll haha.
