About Me

Hi, I'm Chris. I'm recent graduate from the University of North Carolina at Charlotte, I graduated with a bachelor's of science in Computer Science, with a concentration in AI, Robotics and Game Development.

I have a lot of different interests and took a large variety of courses with my time in the education system, anything from business and marketing to sports medicine, psychology, nutrition, biology, art, history and of course, computer science.

Now I'm stuck in this vicious never-ending cycle of learning and creating. Kinda fun though.

More About Me

Career Goals

Right now as a fresh graduate, I understand that no matter how much I've learned in school, there's still and will always be more to learn.

Right now I'm seeking a position where I can grow as a person and a developer and learn from others. Game design and development has and most likely will always be my passion and end goal, but realistically I understand that I need to start somewhere.

My Approach to Projects

I believe in thorough planning, clear communication, and iterative development. I start by understanding the requirements and goals, then break down complex problems into manageable pieces.

Collaboration and communication are extremely important to me. There will always be someone who knows more than you do in a certain area, and I think it's an incredible opportunity to learn from others.

When I'm Not Coding

Gaming is a huge part of my life, I'm thinking about games and theorycrafting and running ideas and numbers in my head all the time. I love to play games, and I love to learn about them.

I also like trying foods from different cultures, and I'm currently trying to learn how to cook. Cooking reminds me of game development - there's a lot of different things you can do and many ways to do it.
